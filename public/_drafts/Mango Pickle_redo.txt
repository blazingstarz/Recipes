## Ingredients

-   1/2 tea spoon cumin seed

-   1/2 tea spoon mustard seeds

-   oil as required

-   1 tea spoon Turmeric powder

-   1 tablespoon garlic crushed 

-   1/2 tea spoon Fenugreek powder 

-   1 1/2 tablespoon mustard powder

-   1 1/2 tablespoon red chilli powder

-   1 raw sour mango/aam

-   3 red chilies dried

-   pinch hing/asafeotida 

-   6 cloves garlic 

-   3 springs curry leaves 

-   salt

## Directions

1. Slice the mango into small cubes and keep it aside.
2. Take a bowl, add red chili powder, mustard powder, fenugreek powder, garlic mashed, turmeric powder, salt and mix this well and keep it aside.
3. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, red chilies, hing, and garlic. Once it is done, switch off the flame and add curry leaves. Keep this tempering aside until it cool down.
4. Now, take spices mixture, add tempering in it and mix it well. Add cut mango cubes into it and mix it well again. Keep this mixture for 24 hours aside.
